About Me

My name is Grace Hinkle, I am 20 and live in boise, Idaho. I am a Junior at Boise State University studying Videogame Development and currently work 3 jobs. I would love to table a booth at AnimeID 2025 using my artist name Tempestspell!


Why I am Applying

I always wanted to sell my own art but was always too scared and confused, but after getting the wonderful opportunity to run a small booth of my own art at the Boise Comic Arts Festival in August of 2024 through BSU, I would love to create art for, apply and run a booth entirely of my own at local conventions. I went to AnimeID last year and really enjoyed it and would love to see it grow in success considering it is the only big anime convention I know of in the area. If given the opportunity I'd love to grow as an artist while also working at something local I want to support.
